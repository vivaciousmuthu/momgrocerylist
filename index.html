Welcome to MGList

About MGList

Tired of messy paper lists and forgotten items?

MGList (Mom's Grocery List) is here to help! We make creating and sharing your shopping list simple, fast, and digital, saving you valuable time. Inspired by a mother's care, our goal is to help you shop smarter for your health and the planet.

Why You'll Love MGList:

  • Build Lists Fast: Quickly select your groceries and prepare your list in seconds, not minutes.
  • Share with Anyone: Instantly send your completed list to your family, friends, or even your local grocery store using WhatsApp, Gmail, Notes, and more.
  • Never Forget Again: Did your mom call with a list while you were busy? She can use MGList to build the list and send it directly to you. No more forgetting items after a workout, a drive, or a long day at work!
  • Perfect for Offline Shoppers: Love the experience of shopping in a store? Ditch the crumpled paper and carry your list cleanly on your phone.
  • Shop with Purpose: A well-planned list helps you buy exactly what you need, promoting wellness and reducing food waste to help our climate.

Meet Your AI Assistants

We've packed powerful AI features into MGList to make your life even easier:

Introducing: Grocery Lens (Pilot Feature)

  • Your Culinary Companion: Have a question about a recipe? Need to know what to cook with the items on your list? Just ask our friendly chatbot! It's your go-to source for food and cooking information. (Please note: Our chatbot is always learning and can sometimes make mistakes. Please double-check critical information.)
  • Grocery Lens (Pilot Feature): Use your phone's camera to take a picture of a handwritten grocery list. Grocery Lens will automatically read the items and add them to your shopping screen. It's the perfect bridge between your old paper notes and your new digital list.

We are passionate about making your life easier. Please try MGList and let us know your valuable feedback as we continue to improve the app for you.
